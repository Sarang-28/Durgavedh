Forts in Pune

1. Sinhagad Fort

Location: Near Pune, Maharashtra

Sinhagad Fort, located near Pune, is one of the most famous forts in Maharashtra. Known for its historical significance, it played a major role in the Maratha Empire's defense. The fort offers scenic views and is a popular destination for trekkers and history enthusiasts.

2. Lohagad Fort

Location: Near Lonavala, Maharashtra

Lohagad Fort is located near Lonavala, Maharashtra. The fort is famous for its massive gates and strategic location, which made it a valuable military base during the Maratha Empire. It is an ideal spot for trekking and offers a panoramic view of the surrounding area.

3. Rajmachi Fort

Location: Near Lonavala, Maharashtra

Rajmachi Fort is a popular fort located near Lonavala. The fort is known for its scenic beauty and historical importance. It is a two-fort complex, with the Shrivardhan and Manaranjan forts being located at the top of a hill. The fort is accessible by a pleasant trek from Lonavala.

4. Torna Fort

Location: Near Pune, Maharashtra

Torna Fort, also known as Prachandagad, is a large fort located near Pune. It is known for its historical significance and strategic location. The fort offers panoramic views of the Sahyadri mountain range and is a popular destination for trekkers and history enthusiasts.

5. Purandar Fort

Location: Near Pune, Maharashtra

Purandar Fort is a historic fort located near Pune. It is known for its twin forts, Purandar and Vajragad (or Rudramal). The fort has played a significant role in the history of the Maratha Empire and offers stunning views of the surrounding landscape.

6. Shivneri Fort

Location: Near Junnar, Maharashtra

Shivneri Fort is a historic fort located near Junnar, Maharashtra. It is known as the birthplace of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, the founder of the Maratha Empire. The fort offers a glimpse into the life and times of Shivaji Maharaj and is a popular destination for history enthusiasts.

7. Rajgad Fort

Location: Near Pune, Maharashtra

Rajgad Fort is a historic fort located near Pune. It is known for its strategic location and historical significance. The fort offers panoramic views of the Sahyadri mountain range and is a popular destination for trekkers and history enthusiasts.

8. Tikona Fort

Location: Near Lonavala, Maharashtra

Tikona Fort is a hill fort located near Lonavala, Maharashtra. The fort is known for its architectural beauty and historical significance. It offers panoramic views of the surrounding area and is a popular destination for trekkers and history enthusiasts.

9. Visapur Fort

Location: Near Lonavala, Maharashtra

Visapur Fort is a hill fort located near Lonavala, Maharashtra. The fort is known for its scenic beauty and historical significance. It offers panoramic views of the surrounding area and is a popular destination for trekkers and history enthusiasts.

10. Tung Fort

Location: Near Lonavala, Maharashtra

Tung Fort is a hill fort located near Lonavala, Maharashtra. The fort is known for its historical significance and panoramic views of the surrounding area. It is a popular destination for trekkers and history enthusiasts.
