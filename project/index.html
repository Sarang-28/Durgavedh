Forts in Maharashtra

1. Raigad Fort

Location: Raigad, Maharashtra (Sahyadri Mountain Range)

Raigad Fort, located in the Sahyadri mountain range, was the capital of the Maratha Empire during the reign of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. It is famous for its impressive architecture and strategic location, offering breathtaking views of the surrounding areas. The fort can be reached by a ropeway or by hiking up the steep path.

2. Sinhagad Fort

Location: Near Pune, Maharashtra

Sinhagad Fort, located near Pune, is one of the most famous forts in Maharashtra. Known for its historical significance, it played a major role in the Maratha Empire's defense. The fort offers scenic views and is a popular destination for trekkers and history enthusiasts.

3. Lohagad Fort

Location: Near Lonavala, Maharashtra

Lohagad Fort is located near Lonavala, Maharashtra. The fort is famous for its massive gates and strategic location, which made it a valuable military base during the Maratha Empire. It is an ideal spot for trekking and offers a panoramic view of the surrounding area.

4. Rajmachi Fort

Location: Near Lonavala, Maharashtra

Rajmachi Fort is a popular fort located near Lonavala. The fort is known for its scenic beauty and historical importance. It is a two-fort complex, with the Shrivardhan and Manaranjan forts being located at the top of a hill. The fort is accessible by a pleasant trek from Lonavala.
